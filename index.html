About us

Make ordering food in Food Court Easier

Order's UP is a company engaged in technology. Our business model is Business to Business (B2B). The products we offer are mobile applications to facilitate ordering food and drinks at the food court mall.

Our Business Model Canvas

Our Goal

We want to simplify transaction processes in food courts.
Customer doesn't need to queue when they order and make a trip back to the restaurant to take their food.
They can order using our app and the food will be delivered to their table

Our Product

Splash Screen

The first page that customer seen. Showing our proud logo and searching customer location in background.

Scan QR Code Screen

Customer scan barcode which provided in each table. So we know where they seat and deliver their food easily

Restaurant List Screen

Customer can see all restaurant in food court they currently at.

Menu List Screen

Customer can see all menu which restaurant they choose.

Order Summary Screen

Customer can see all food they ordered and how much they need to pay to each restaurant.
